About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Economics at the University of Southern California. Before I was at the Toulouse School of Economics where I did my masters in Decision Mathematics.

I work on Economics and Game Theory, where I am particularly interested in the economics of data. My research questions include, what is the value of data, how do you buy/elicit data from consumers and what are the incentives of consumers in the market for information.
